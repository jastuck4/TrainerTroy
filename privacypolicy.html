Privacy Policy

Information you provide to us directly:

We ask for certain information such as your first and last name, email address, and gender. We may keep any messages you send through the Contact form. We use this information to improve customer service, our website, and for client convience. Your information is not published publicly.

Sharing your information:

We will not rent, sell, or give any of your information to any parties outside of TrainerTroy.com

Who we may share your information with:

We may share your information with third-party business partners for the sole purpose of providing service to you. Third parties include those used for payment transactions. We will require that any third party fully comply with this Privacy Policy or operate under a privacy policy that is similar.

Instances where we are required to share your information:

We will disclose your information if and when required by law or subpoena.

Analytics Information:

We utilize third party analytics to directly collect analytics data. The data that is collected helps us to measure website traffic and to detail usage trends. The analytics tool collects information sent through your browser to include the pages you visit and how often.

Information Compromise:

We do our best to ensure that the your information collected through our site remains secure. If this information is compromised due to a breach of security, we will take the necessary steps to investigate the situation and if need be, notify you in accordance with any laws and regulations.

Third-party links:

When you use a link from this website to visit another, our Privacy Policy does not apply to that site. Any activity you conduct on the third-party site is subject to that site's Privacy Policy.
