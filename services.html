Services

I incorporate various workout styles into each workout plan, depending on your specific goals. Below are two trainings that I offer in addition to the general fitness training sessions.


Crossfit Dynamics Training

In crossfit dynamics training, you will learn the fundamentals of crossfit lifting. Lifts that are incorporated into the training are power cleans, clean and jerks, back squats, front squats, thrusters, shoulder press, and deadlifts (don't worry, it isn't all cramped in one session).

Weightlifting Training

In weightlifting training, I teach clients the fundamentals of weightlifting. This includes lifting techniques for bench pressing and simple dumbbell lifts It is vital that if you incorporate lifting into your workouts that you learn to do the exercises properly to prevent injury.

These training sessions are only scheduled separately from the general fitness training sessions.

Nutrition Guidance

I also offer each of my clients guidance in what diet is best suited for them. When training your body, it is important to learn what is best to put into your body. Once you have reached your fitness goals, it becomes all about maintaining and one of the top ways to accomplish that is to maintain a healthy diet.

Making fitness apart of your lifestyle involves taking action in and outside of the gym.
