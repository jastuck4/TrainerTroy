About Me

My name is Troy Tucker and I'm a personal trainer.

I specialize in crossfit training and weightlifting, but also incorporate combinations of other types of workouts into my training plans. I hold certifications in CPR, nutrition, and of course personal training. It is my belief that fitness should be apart of our daily lifestyle and not just as apart of a dieting routine. It is also my belief that everyone has different levels of fitness potential, which is why I formulate workout plans to fit each individual client.
